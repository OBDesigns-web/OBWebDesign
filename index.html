Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. A simple landing page can be done in a week, while a full e-commerce site might take 4-6 weeks. We'll give you a timeline during our initial consultation.

How much does a website cost?

Our projects start at $1,500 for a basic website. Pricing depends on features, pages, and complexity. Check out our pricing page for detailed packages or contact us for a custom quote.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all devicesâ€”phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile-first design is a core part of our development process.

Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

Yes! We offer hosting and monthly maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and running smoothly. We can also set you up with your own hosting if you prefer to manage it yourself.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Yes! We can integrate a CMS (Content Management System) that makes it easy for you to update text, images, and content without any coding knowledge. We also provide training after launch.
