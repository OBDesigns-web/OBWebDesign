Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us.

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple landing page typically takes 1-2 weeks, a business website 2-4 weeks, and larger projects like e-commerce stores can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

To get started, we'll need your logo (if you have one), brand colors, content (text and images), and any examples of websites you like. Don't worry if you don't have everything ready—we can help guide you through the process and even assist with content creation.

All our packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions. We work closely with you throughout the design process to ensure you're happy with the direction before moving to development. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds can be accommodated at an hourly rate.

Yes! We offer optional hosting and maintenance packages to keep your site running smoothly. This includes security updates, backups, uptime monitoring, and minor content updates. We can also help you set up your own hosting if you prefer to manage it yourself.

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion before the final handoff. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal.
